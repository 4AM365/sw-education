sRGB - pretty much everything is sRGB

DCI-P3 (Digital Cinema) is a standard from the film industry that gives you a more saturated color gamut, and is explicitly used for content created and tagged with that standard. Netflix might use it. Anything that's HDR will use it. 

Display P3 (Apple) used for consumer displays - this is most likely what the BenQ is.

HDR - greater dynamic brightness range. Windows has an HDR mode that's enabled with win+alt+b

Windows HDR - enables HDR globally, needs to be toggled on before viewing HDR content and off after
